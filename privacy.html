Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. This policy explains how we protect your pharmaceutical verification data.

Introduction

At MediSentry, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our pharmaceutical verification services.

By accessing or using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We never sell or monetize your personal data.

What Data We Collect

We collect different types of information for verification and reporting purposes:

Medicine Verification Data

When you verify a medicine, we collect the product name, batch number, and expiration date to process your request.

Report Information

If you report a suspected counterfeit, we collect details about the medicine, packaging, and your observations.

Optional Contact Information

You may choose to provide your name and email if you want follow-up on a report or verification.

Technical Data

We automatically collect IP address, browser type, and device information for security and analytics.

How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us provide and improve our pharmaceutical safety services:

Medicine Authentication

To verify pharmaceutical products against manufacturer databases and identify potential counterfeits.

Public Health Reporting

Aggregated, anonymous data helps regulatory bodies track counterfeit medicine trends.

Service Improvement

Analyze usage patterns to enhance our verification algorithms and user experience.

What We Never Do
  • Sell or rent your personal information to third parties
  • Use your data for marketing purposes without explicit consent
  • Share identifiable information with pharmaceutical companies

Anonymous Reporting

If you choose to report anonymously, we collect no personal data from you. Anonymous reports help:

Track Counterfeits

Identify geographical clusters of fake medicines without personal identifiers.

Protect Whistleblowers

Enable sensitive reporting without compromising your identity.

Data Storage & Security

We implement robust measures to protect your pharmaceutical data:

Encryption Standards

All data transmissions use TLS 1.3 encryption, and stored data is protected with AES-256 encryption.

Access Controls

Strict role-based access ensures only authorized personnel can view sensitive information.

Regular Audits

Independent security audits verify our compliance with global data protection standards.

Compliance Certifications: MediSentry maintains ISO 27001 certification for information security management and complies with GDPR principles for data protection.

Your Rights

You have control over your pharmaceutical verification data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Right to Erasure

Ask us to delete your personal information from our systems.

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We'll notify you of significant changes through our website or email.

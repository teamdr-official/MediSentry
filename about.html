About MediSentry

Fighting Counterfeit Medicines with Technology

An initiative by Chandigarh University students to make medicine authentication accessible, reliable, and citizen-driven

Our Mission The Problem
Our Mission

Empowering Citizens Against Counterfeit Drugs

"Don't guess, verify first."

We are dedicated to creating a safer healthcare ecosystem by providing tools and knowledge to detect counterfeit medicines. Our platform integrates cutting-edge technology with community participation to combat this growing public health threat.

  • Real-time medicine verification using batch codes and packaging details
  • Community-driven reporting system for suspicious drugs
  • Educational resources to recognize counterfeit medications
  • Data analytics to identify counterfeit hotspots
The Challenge

The Growing Threat of Counterfeit Medicines

India faces a serious public health crisis from fake, substandard, or expired drugs circulating in the market

10%+

of medicines in developing countries are counterfeit according to WHO estimates

1M+

deaths annually are linked to fake medicines as reported by The Lancet

72%

$30B

global counterfeit drug market causing economic losses

Consequences of Counterfeit Drugs

  • Failed treatments and worsening health conditions
  • Increased antimicrobial resistance
  • Irreversible harm or death
  • Erosion of trust in healthcare systems
Our Solution

How MediSentry Works

An interactive health-tech ecosystem designed for public protection

Medicine Verification

Check authenticity using batch codes, manufacturer details, or uploaded packaging images with our comprehensive database.

Report Counterfeit

Anonymous reporting system that triggers alerts to authorities and warns other users about suspicious medications.

Awareness Center

Interactive guides, comparison tools, and quizzes to help identify fake packaging and understand drug safety.

AI Detection

Future implementation of machine learning for image-based fraud detection and pattern recognition in counterfeit reports.
